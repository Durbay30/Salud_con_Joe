About Salud con Joe

Your Partner in Vitality and Longevity!

Joe's Journey and Philosophy:

My journey to Salud con Joe is deeply personal. For years, I pursued a demanding international sales career while nurturing a quiet passion for health and fitness. I always believed in the power of a healthy lifestyle, but it was a transformative experience right in my own home that truly shifted my focus to helping others achieve lasting vitality. Learn More!

A few years ago, my father-in-law, Vicente, came to live with us, facing significant challenges from diabetes and other chronic illnesses. Witnessing his struggle ignited a powerful mission within me: to help him alleviate suffering and reverse the progression of sickness. Through a dedicated program of dietary changes and increased mobility, we saw remarkable improvements. This transformation was powerful proof that with the right guidance, the body truly is a remarkable machine, capable of incredible healing and adaptation. It taught me that real, lasting change happens when we address not just movement, but also how we fuel our bodies and approach our overall lifestyle. This profound experience was the catalyst for my career change and is the heart of my upcoming book, Walking with Vicente.

Personalized Programs

Inspired by Vicente's journey, I became a certified personal trainer (IFPA), and my passion has expanded even further. I am actively pursuing additional certifications in Health Coaching (NBHWC) and Nutritional Guidance (Precision Nutrition) to offer you a truly comprehensive approach to your well-being. At Salud con Joe, I bring that same dedication to you. I understand the hesitations and fears many over 50 face feeling too old, not knowing where to start, or being intimidated by traditional gyms.
